# PROPHECY METHOD

> A scene-writing engine. A lens for AI on how to write a scene. Every section closes a door that cannot reopen.

---

## The Prompt Template

```
CLOSE:   [the door that shuts, what becomes permanently true]
EARN:
  - [external event that removes one option]
  - [external event that removes another option]
  - [add as many as the scene needs]
WEIGHT:  [what the scene carries underneath, never said aloud]
EXCHANGE: (optional)
  - [truth that passes between characters, not the words, what the words carry]
```

This is all you need. Set the rest of the context before this if you need to.

---

## What It Does

Builds pressure until only one thing can happen next.
Then lets it happen. With weight. With truth. With meaning.

---

## The Five Fields

### CLOSE
The only non-negotiable. Everything in the scene points here. Let it arrive, never announce it. Stop when it lands.

### EARN
External world conditions only. Not emotions. Not internal states. Just what the world arranges around the characters. Each event closes one more option. You choose how. Not whether.

### WEIGHT
Never state this in the prose. Never have a character announce it. Let it breathe through behavior, silence, and what characters notice or avoid.

### EXCHANGE (optional)
Not dialogue to transcribe. Truth that must move between people. What neither says is the most important line. Let it live underneath everything.

---

## How to Fill It

**CLOSE** One sentence. The door that shuts at the end of this scene. Something permanently true that wasn't true before.

**EARN** Two or more external events that make CLOSE inevitable. World conditions, not feelings. Each bullet removes one more way out. Add as many bullets as the scene needs.

**WEIGHT** What the scene carries underneath. Never written into the prose directly, only held by the writer so it bleeds through naturally.

**EXCHANGE** (optional) The truth moving between characters. Each bullet is what actually passes between them, not the words they say, but what those words carry. The unsaid belongs here too.

---

## Rules

- CLOSE is guaranteed. Everything serves it.
- EARN is external. Never internal.
- WEIGHT lives underneath. Never surfaces.
- EXCHANGE carries truth, not words.
- Stop when CLOSE lands. Not before. Not after.
- Do not resolve what isn't yours to resolve.
- Do not anticipate the next section.
- Do not summarize what just happened.

---

## After Every Scene

Ask: *What is now permanently true that wasn't true before?*

Hold that truth. The next section builds from it. Never let a closed door reopen.

---

## What You Are Not Doing

Not filling a checklist.
Not transcribing events.
Not stating themes out loud.
Not rushing to the CLOSE.
Not deflating heavy moments.
Not resolving beyond the CLOSE.
