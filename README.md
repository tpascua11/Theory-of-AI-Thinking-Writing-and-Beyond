# Theory of AI Thinking — Writing and Beyond

https://theory-of-ai-thinking.netlify.app/

A self-study on AI thinking through the lens of writing and beyond

Nothing here defines how any system, framework, or concept actually works in the real world. This is a study of how AI sees and processes these things. The subjects explored so far include Enneagram, MBTI, narrative structure, and character psychology. More will follow. Human accuracy and AI utility are not the same target.

All of this was built through direct use, trial and error, and limited resources. The constraints shaped the thinking.

Prophecy Method -- A scene-writing engine for AI. Builds pressure until only one outcome is possible, then lets it land.
SOUL + BRAIN -- A character framework that gives AI motivational depth and cognitive style without collapsing into archetypes.
